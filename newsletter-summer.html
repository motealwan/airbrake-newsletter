Airbrake Gem - 4.0

We've made a major bump to our Airbrake Gem to 4.0 Ruby Gem, We've been mainly pulling of Javascript tracking out of the Gem to provide better support using Airbrake JS.


Airbrake iOS 4.0 and app de-symbolicating beta

We've bumped out iOS notifier to version 4.0. It has a bunch of bug fixes and improvments to increase the speed and added a few more features, check the Changelog for more details.

The re-write includes server-side de-symbolication processing .dysm files to provide useful backtraces. To get early access to the new notifier with de-symbolicating, Email support@airbrake.io



New! Slack integration.

We've created three new error classes. These will affect how errors are grouped and how notifications are sent. They are managed on a per project level.
